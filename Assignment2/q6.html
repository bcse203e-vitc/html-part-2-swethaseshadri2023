Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta with garlic until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix with pancetta.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture (off the heat).
  6. Season with black pepper and serve hot.

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 400 per serving

Protein: 20g | Carbs: 50g | Fat: 10g

Fun Fact: Carbonara originates from central Italy!
